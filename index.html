Recipe of Barbecue Chicken Sandwiches

Why Barbecue Chicken Sandwiches?

It is always good to have your favorite food. What is more than good to have your favorite food? It is by making it yourself. Barbecue Chicken Sandwich seems so difficult to make but in fact, this is super easy as you can't image. This dish can be your breakfast, brunch, lunch, afternoon tea, dinner or even late night supper. Ready for this cooking adventure? Here you go!

The Ingredients and Amounts

  • 4 med. chickens
  • 1 (29 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (18 oz.) bottle Bullseye Hickory smoke sauce
  • 1 (18 oz.) bottle reg. flavor barbecue sauce
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons fried chicken seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon garlic
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon celery salt
  • cup honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

The Preparation Instructions

  1. Boil 4 medium chickens until tender, cool, remove skin from chicken and pull chicken from bone.
  2. Place in crock pot and pour barbecue sauce over chicken.
  3. Turn crock pot on high until chicken and sauce comes to a boil, ready to serve.
  4. Serve on buns.
  5. BARBECUE SAUCE: Mix remaining ingredients together and pour over the 4 medium chickens that have been pulled from the bone.
